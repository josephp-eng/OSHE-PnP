Hello!



Each subsystem is organized in the proceeding folders. Various STL and STEP files are available here for the current version of the pick and place machine. These are available for 3D printing in your choice of slicer or for contributing or modifying the project. Many models were produced in Fusion 360 and SolidWorks by the talented OSHE Pick and Place team. Enjoy!



##### Printing Guide



It is recommended to print almost everything in PLA. Gyroid infill is the best pattern for strength on a majority of these parts. The Ender 3 LCD back cover can be found on thingiverse. Make sure to print two rod holders.

* Print with a 0.4mm nozzle
* At least 15% infill
* 0.2mm layers
* Tree supports (organic if available)
* 2-3 wall loops



PushPullFeeder is recommended to print in PETG for durability and flexibility. Our model is modified from makr.zone, using similar print settings found there. Included is the OpenSCAD file as well as printable STLs.

* Print with a 0.4mm nozzle
* 20% infill
* 0.24mm layers
* Triangles
* 3 wall loops
* Normal supports, on build plate only
* Recommended 2 skirt loops
